Car-Parrinello molecular dynamics (CPMD)¶

The method is due to two Italian physicists, Michele Parrinello and Roberto Car who published the method in their article titled Unified Approach for Molecular Dynamics and Density Functional Theory in 1985. Parrinello is also known for the Parrinello-Rahman barostat (with Aneesur Rahman) and Metadynamics (with Alessandro Laio.
